My research

My research interests are extra-galactic high energy transients, including gamma-ray burst, binary neutron star merger magnetar powered X-ray emissions, SN shock breakouts and tidal disruption events, etc.

  • The detections of transients depend sensitively on the field of view of the telescopes. Especially, the study of X-ray transients is only in its babyhood. I have been working on the study of the luminosity functions and event rate densities of these transients from observations. The results can be essential to understanding the progenitor systems of these transients and their cosmological evolution.
