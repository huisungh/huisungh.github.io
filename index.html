About me

Research Assistant
Key Laboratory of Space Astronomy and Technology
National Astronomical Observatories, CAS

I am a research assistant at the National Astronomical Observatories (NAO), Chinese Academy of Sciences. Before that, I stayed at NAOC as a post-doc during the years of 2017-2021. I received my Ph.D. degree from Peking University in 2017, under the supervision of Professor Bing Zhang and Zhuo Li. I stayed as an exchange student at University of Nevada, Las Vegas during 2014-2015 and late 2016.
I am also a team member of Einstein Probe, which is an X-ray mission with a wide field X-ray telescope (F.O.V ~ 3600 square degrees) and a follow-up X-ray telescope onboard and plan to launch by the late 2023.
