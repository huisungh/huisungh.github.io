About me

I am a research assistant at the National Astronomical Observatories, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests are extra-galactic high energy transients, e.g., gamma-ray burst, binary neutron star merger magnetar powered X-ray emissions, SN shock breakouts and tidal disruption events. I am now a team member of Einstein Probe Science Center.
